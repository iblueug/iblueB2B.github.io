Frequently Asked Questions

🛒 Buying Questions

How do I contact a seller?

You can contact sellers through:

  • Chat: Click the chat icon on the listing page
  • Phone: Call directly if the seller has shared their number
  • WhatsApp: Many sellers prefer WhatsApp - look for the WhatsApp button

Always communicate through iBlue's platform until you're ready to meet.

How can I tell if a seller is trustworthy?

Look for these trust indicators:

  • ✅ Verified badge on the seller's profile
  • ⭐ Positive reviews from other buyers
  • 📅 Member for a long time
  • 📸 Clear, original photos of products
  • 💬 Responsive to messages

When in doubt, meet in a public place and inspect items before paying.

What should I do if an item is not as described?

If you receive an item that doesn't match the description:

  1. Contact the seller immediately through iBlue chat
  2. Take photos showing the issue
  3. Try to reach a resolution (return/refund)
  4. If no resolution, report to iBlue support within 48 hours

We'll investigate and take appropriate action.

💰 Selling Questions

How do I post an ad?

Posting an ad is easy:

  1. Click the + Post Ad button (bottom right on mobile)
  2. Choose a category for your item
  3. Add clear photos (at least 3 recommended)
  4. Write a descriptive title and detailed description
  5. Set your price and location
  6. Review and publish

Your ad will be live after quick review (usually within minutes).

How can I make my ad more visible?

Boost your ad visibility with these tips:

  • Use quality photos: Clear, well-lit images from multiple angles
  • Write detailed descriptions: Include specs, condition, reason for selling
  • Price competitively: Research similar items
  • Promote your ad: Use our Boost feature (starts at UGX 5,000)
  • Share on social media: Use the share button
  • Refresh your ad: Bump it every few days
When will I get paid?

Payment timing depends on the method:

  • Cash on pickup: Immediate
  • Mobile Money: Instant
  • Bank transfer: 1-2 business days
  • iBlue Escrow: Released after buyer confirms receipt

Always confirm payment before releasing items.

👤 Account Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email or phone number
  4. Check your email/SMS for reset code
  5. Enter the code and create new password

If you don't receive the code, check spam folder or contact support.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account permanently:

  1. Go to Settings > Account
  2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your decision
  4. Enter your password

Note: This action cannot be undone. Your listings will be removed, and you'll lose all data. For help, email privacy@iblue.ug

🛡️ Safety Questions

How do I report a suspicious user?

To report a user:

  1. Go to their profile or listing
  2. Click the ⋯ (three dots) menu
  3. Select "Report User"
  4. Choose the reason and provide details

Our safety team will review within 24 hours. In emergencies, contact Uganda Police.

What are common scams to watch out for?

Common scams in Uganda include:

  • Advance payment: Buyer asks for deposit before seeing item
  • Overpayment: "Buyer" sends too much and asks for refund
  • Fake delivery: "Seller" asks for shipping fee but never sends item
  • Phishing: Messages asking for your password or PIN

🚨 Never share your password, PIN, or send money to strangers.

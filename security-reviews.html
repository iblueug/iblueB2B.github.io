    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I delete my account?

    Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. You can also email support@iblue.ug with your request. We'll process it within 7 days and confirm via email.

    Is my payment information secure?

    Yes. We use PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. Your full payment details never touch our servers. All transactions are encrypted with 256-bit SSL.

    Do you share my data with third parties?

    Only with trusted service providers who help operate our platform, and only when necessary. We never sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

    How long do you keep my data?

    We retain data as long as your account is active. After deletion, we keep necessary information for legal purposes (up to 7 years for transaction records).

    What happens if there's a data breach?

    We'll notify you within 72 hours if your data is affected, explain what happened, and guide you on protective steps. We also report to Uganda's data protection authority as required by law.
